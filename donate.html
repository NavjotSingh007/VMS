ANNUAL FUND

Your donations make up about 10% of VMS’ operating budget, providing support for the core programs that inspire students to achieve more, strive more, learn more, and ultimately, act more for the good of all. - See more at: https://www.vms.edu/giveonline#sthash.7ZBqsBcn.dpuf

You may direct your gifts to the areas that you find most inspiring:

  • Curriculum Enhancement
  • Technology
  • Scholarships
  • Faculty Development
  • Athletics
  • Arts
  • Area of Greatest Need

The VMS community prepares our children intellectually, emotionally, and ethically to thrive in a college setting and beyond. We provide students with the life-long purpose of active participation in global citizenship. Thank you for making VMS a place to be inspired and, “Learn more.”
